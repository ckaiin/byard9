General Rules of Play

Equipment Regulation

Each player is permitted to carry and use a single (1) club for the duration of the round. The selected club must fall within the range of a 6-iron through a 60-degree wedge, inclusive. Clubs outside this specification — including but not limited to long irons, hybrids, fairway woods, and putters — are not permitted. Players must declare their club selection prior to commencing play on Hole 1.

Players must play the ball as it lies unless relief is explicitly permitted under the applicable hole regulation.
Maximum stroke limits apply on every hole. A player's score may not exceed the stated maximum regardless of strokes taken.
All penalty drops result in a one-stroke addition (+1) to the player's score unless otherwise specified in the hole regulation.
Each player is responsible for maintaining an accurate and honest stroke count throughout the round.
All disputes shall be settled by majority ruling among the players present at the time of the dispute.

Bucket Target · Bonus Hole

The ball must strike the bucket or come to rest within one grip length of the bucket using the player's club. If the ball lands inside the bucket, the player receives a one-stroke bonus (–1). The elevated surface may not be moved.

Circle or Sign
